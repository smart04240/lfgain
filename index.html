How do I claim my reward?

You do not need to claim your reward manually. Your rewards will be automatically sent to your wallet after every reward cycle. You can view information about your rewards and other statistics through our dashboard.

How often do I receive rewards?

You will receive your rewards approximately every 24 hours although there are plans to reduce this down to 1 hour in the future. After connecting your wallet in the dashboard, you will be able to see more detailed information about your rewards.

How do I re-invest my rewards to get more XLD tokens?

Through the dashboard you can allocate a portion of your rewards for re-investment. You only need to adjust the slider according to your preferences and the rest will be taken care of automatically. By default, 100% of your rewards will be sent out in BNB.

What is the reward pool?

The reward pool contains the total accumulation of BNB that come from the 12% tax applied by the contract. The reward pool increases whenever a transaction is made and decreases whenever a user claims their share.

My claim date got extended?

There are two cases when your claim date can be extended. The first one occurs when you buy or sell more than 90% of the amount of $XLD tokens you own. The other case occurs when you sell all of your $XLD tokens and then buy back some more. Check out the reward penalties section of our documentation for more information.

Why is my reward rate less/more that it was yesterday?

The amount of BNB you receive depends on the amount of $XLD tokens you own and the size of the reward pool. Since the reward pool constantly changes in size by people receiving their rewards and taxes being applied, the amount you receive also changes accordingly.

How can I buy $XLD?

You can either participate in the presale once it starts or purchase $XLD tokens from PancakeSwap V2 when the presale ends. Please visit our documentation for more detailed steps.

What is the recommended amount of slippage I should use when buying $XLD tokens through PancakeSwap?

~18% when buying
~26% when selling

Is liquidity locked and for how long?

100% of the LP tokens will be locked after the presale ends for 1 year.

What are the plans for the future?

The LFGAIN team will work closely with the community to promote the $XLD ecosystem continuously as time goes on. This includes various marketing plans, partnerships and app development. Check out our documentation for more information.
