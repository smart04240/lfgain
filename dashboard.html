Reinvest your rewards

You can choose to allocate a portion of your rewards to be automatically re-invested after every cycle. The XLD tokens will be sent automatically to your wallet. To learn more about how this works check out our docs.

For example, you can choose to convert 10% of your rewards to XLD. After every reward cycle, the XLD will appear automatically into your wallet, along with the remaining 90% as BNB.

By moving the slider towards XLD you are increasing your allocated portion that will be converted to XLD.
